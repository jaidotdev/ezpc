History

EasyPC, founded in the Philippines, aims to provide quality computer components and accessories to tech enthusiasts and professionals. With multiple branches and an online store, the company offers competitive pricing, diverse products, and excellent customer service. EasyPC is a go-to destination for PC builders, gamers, and IT professionals, empowering the local tech community.
